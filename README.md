Financial Security & Legal Engineering Research Archive
This repository contains research materials, datasets, and methodological frameworks developed by Professor Anton Chubenko, S.J.D.

Research Focus
Financial Security: Legal mechanisms for preventing money laundering (AML/CFT) and financial crimes.

Asset Recovery: Strategic frameworks for cross-border asset tracing and restitution.

Cultural Heritage Protection: Legal engineering for the protection and recovery of Ukrainian cultural property.

Veteran Reintegration: Projects related to the VetFinHub initiative.
